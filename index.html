About me


My name is Tanner Austin, I am a 27 year old Engineer living in Austin, Texas. I graduated from Weber State in Manufacturing Engineering, and worked as a robotics engineer for two years. I knew software was my ultimate goal, and I had slowly began to teach myself during my time in school. However, it wasn't until the Covid-19 pandemic hit that I realized I needed to jump into this market ASAP.
I signed up for a coding boot camp at the University of Texas at Austin, and began really pushing myself to learn as much as I could. My goal is to become a full-stack developer, with a background in manufacturing and robotics.

I love being out on the water, either paddle boarding on Ladybird Lake or boating on Lake Travis. During the few winter months in Austin, you can find me playing video games or building computers.

Some of my favorite things about Austin are going to the UT football games, driving through the hill country and trying new places to eat constantly!
